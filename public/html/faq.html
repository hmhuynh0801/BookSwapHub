You have to sign up for the account with your college E-mail to be able to swap books

How to add your book to offer to swap?

Add the book you have on the add book page and put in what kind of book that you are looking for. when someone is looking for the book that you have. and they also have the book that you are looking for. they will e-mail you to swap the books.

search for books

You also can look up on the find book page with the ISBN code to search for the book that you are looking for and you also have the book that person is looking for. So, you just e-mail the owner of the book to swap books with them.

Rules for swap books

To be able to swap books, Both of the user have to exchange their student ID that have their college name on it. Please also make sure both users agree on the time of that they will return each other books
